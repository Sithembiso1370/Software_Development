The Schools History

Reagile Primary School is a caring and friendly Primary School situated in Winnie Mandela Park, Tembisa, just behind Phumulani Mall, Olifantsfontein. Founded in 12/31/1899, Reagile Primary School is a mixed-gender / co-education, uniform is their pride and joy. The school has high expectations of wearing right uniform clolours, with all clothing to be worn correctly and cleaned frequently.
