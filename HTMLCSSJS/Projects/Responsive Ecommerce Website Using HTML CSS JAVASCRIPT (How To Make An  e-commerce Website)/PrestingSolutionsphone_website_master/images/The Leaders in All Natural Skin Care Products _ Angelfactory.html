Welcome to Angelfactory

Our skincare, body care, and bath products combine carrier oils, clays, teas, extracts, and essential oils of the highest quality to bring you ultimate nutrition for your skin. We have a product that suits every skin type and for every need, your skin might have. Our products are simple,  yet sophisticated. Each product is carefully blended from the highest quality ingredients to nourish your skin from the inside out. Our specialized choice of oils makes it highly penetrable,  so nutrients can be delivered deep into your skin. Non-Greasy, yet nourishing, it gives your skin a beautiful healthy glow. Angelfactory Skincare contains no chemicals, colourants, or fragrances. We do not test our products on animals. We support local and sustainable farming practices to bring you skincare that is truly guilt-free.
